Gajar ka halwa

Recipe:-

Gajar ka halwa or carrot halwa is favourite across the country, this Indian dessert Gajar Ka Halwa is a delicious blend of carrot (gajar) with milk and sugar in desi ghee that adds to the aroma and flavour. An easy recipe that is usually made in winters when the carrots are in season. This Sunehri style gajar ka halwa is Pakistani style with grated carrot and condensed milk pudding. as it taste so good that no can control themselves from eating once tatsted specially the sweet lovers like me,
